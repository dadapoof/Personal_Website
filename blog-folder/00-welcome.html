Welcome To David's Thoughts

Posted by David, April 12, 2018 | Last Updated: April 12th, 2018.



Welcome to my blog! Wow. Took me a while to get this set up, but we are finally here. Reasons why I decided to create this blog:

1. I like to write. Despite my horrendous style of writing, I actually enjoy tapping furiously on keys to produce coherrent sentences and have other people read them. Bare with me.

2. I can now express my thoughts and opinions about both CS and non-CS related topics! I've been wanting a place to talk about coding and regular news, and what better way to do that than by creating a blog.

3. I can now finally say: "Oh, I wrote an article about that in my blog". Coolness level: Off the charts.

4. What the hell. Life's short. Might as well record it somehow.



What you can expect from the blog and what I'll be writing about:

1. Computer Science Related Topics. This will range from opinion pieces on what is happening in the media, to opinion pieces on technologies, to tutorials on CS stuff.

2. Non-Computer Science Related Topics. Basically, everything else.

3. Short Stories And Things Like That. I want to start writing stories that are most likely going to be fiction.

4. Personal Life. Updates on my personal life, my career, and my goals for the future!

5. Travel Experiences. This one is self explanatory.

6. Life Experiences. Advice for others based on what I've experienced.

7. Reviews Of Products Or Books/Movies. I really like reading books and I would love to do a couple of reviews!

8. So Much More. Literally, anything that comes to mind!



So make sure to check out the rest of my articles! I try to create wholesome content while keeping myself amused. I plan on writing one article a week and hopefully I will be able to keep that up for a while. In the meantime, Email me with any questions, concerns, or comments! I'd love to chat with almost anyone...

~ David
