Sadly, despite being in this club for my four years of highschool, I still thought I wanted to become a Biology major in college and do Bio-med. Moreover, I worked closely with a French speaking student who needed the whole class to be translated to him.

However, it was only when I took my first Computer Science course in college that I decided to major in it. During my first semester at Tufts University, I am taking Comp 11, an introduction to C++. After one month of Comp 11, I am already able to create games like Mastermind, the dice game called Pig, Hangman, and the famous game 2048. Moreover, I am enrolled in the next CS course next semester, COMP15 where we learn about data structures and algorithms. I am very excited to put my C++ code to use and expand my skills to other coding languages. Finally, I am constantly seeking to create projects with the new knowledge I aquire. This personal website is an example of that!
